The MK2 GTI

The GTI MK2 was the successor to the MK1 Golf GTI. It debuted in 1985 with the same 1.8L engine used on the MK1 GTI. The styling was also kept similar, with some differences being a different rear end and some slightly smoother lines. In 1986, a GTI known as the GTI 16V was introduced. This GTI had a 16 valve 1.8L engine which brought the power up to 137 horsepower and 124 lb-ft. of torque.
