The MK1 GTI

The Golf GTI MK1 debuted in March of 1975 in Europe, after it had been in secret development by a small team at Volkswagen. The team took an existing first generation Golf and gave it a bigger fuel-injected engine, stiffer suspension, and anti-rollbars in the front and rear. The engine was married to a 4 speed gearbox, but in 1979 was replaced in favor of a 5 speed manual transmission. The car was released in North America in 1983, 8 years after its initial launch.
